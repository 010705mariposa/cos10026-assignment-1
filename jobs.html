Data Scientist

Location

Position Title

As a Data Scientist at Cooker, you will be a key member of our data team, driving impactful insights and innovation through advanced analytics. In this dynamic role, you will contribute to shaping our data strategy and leveraging cutting-edge technologies to solve complex business challenges. Your responsibilities will encompass:

  • Conducting in-depth analysis of large and diverse datasets to uncover hidden patterns, trends, and opportunities.
  • Designing, developing, and implementing sophisticated statistical models and machine learning algorithms to extract actionable insights.
  • Collaborating with cross-functional teams to identify business requirements and develop data-driven solutions that enhance decision-making processes.
  • Translating complex technical findings into clear and compelling insights for non-technical stakeholders.
  • Staying abreast of the latest advancements in statistics, machine learning, and data science, and applying them to enhance our analytical capabilities.
  • Contributing to the development and maintenance of scalable and efficient data pipelines.

The ideal candidate for this role should possess a passion for data-driven decision-making, a deep understanding of statistical concepts, and the ability to communicate complex ideas effectively. Additionally, exceptional programming skills in languages such as Python or R, coupled with strong communication and collaboration skills, will be crucial for success in this position.

If you thrive in a fast-paced and innovative environment, are eager to tackle challenging problems, and have a track record of delivering impactful insights, we encourage you to apply and join our team of data enthusiasts at Cooker.

$80,000 - $100,000 per years

Vu Minh Tuan

  • Develop and implement machine learning models for predictive analysis.
  • Analyze and interpret large datasets to discover trends and patterns.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to design and implement data-driven solutions.

  • Proficiency in Python and R for data analysis and machine learning.
  • Minimum of 5 years of experience in a data science role.

  • Experience with big data technologies (e.g., Hadoop, Spark).
